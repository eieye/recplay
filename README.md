# Experimental Rec(order)Play(er) with MediaStream Recording API<br>
(loosely based on "Web Dictaphone" https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/MediaStream_Recording_API/Using_the_MediaStream_Recording_API)<br>
&nbsp;<br>
Allow use of the microphone in the browser pop-up ...<br>
• "Listen" to hear audio template/prompt<br>
• "StartREC" to record yourself repeating the prompt<br>
• "StopREC" when your done<br>
• "PlayREC" to listen to your recording<br>
(repeat StartREC to overwrite your previous recording)<br>
&nbsp;<br>
Yellow bar visualizes input level (volume).<br>
Blue bar represents the recorded audio track – after(!) first recording.<br>
&nbsp;<br>
Move your finger (touchscreen) or drag pointer on the blue bar for synchronous playback ("scrubbing").<br>
Recording can be saved with "Download REC" (.ogg file)<br>
&nbsp;<br>
Demo: https://www.jenskreitmeyer.de/alphasprint/RECPLAY_API/recplay.html<br>
